__**hello**__
    syntax:
        `>polybot hello`
    say hello to polybot
__**thanks**__
    syntax:
        `>polybot thanks`
    thank polybot for doing such a good job!
__**help**__
    syntax:
        `>polybot help`
    display this help message
__**greet**__
    syntax:
        `>polybot greet [ping]`
    ask polybot to greet a user you ping
__**add**__
    syntax:
        `>polybot add [relationship type] [ping]`
    add a user to a relationship, used for requesting and confirming relationships. Current relationship types are QPP, FWB, and partner
__**status**__
    syntax:
        `>polybot status [ping]`
    check your relationship status with a person
__**remove**__
    syntax:
        `>polybot remove [ping]`
    remove a relationship with a person
__**display**__
    syntax:
        `>polybot display`
    display your polycule
